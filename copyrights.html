Who we are

Our website address is https://indusdraw.tech. IndusDraw is a project of the Takama Laboratory, Tokyo Metropolitan University.

What personal data do we collect and why we collect it

We use cookies with embedded additional third-party content and monitor your interaction to collect your personal information. When you visit the website, you can draw and download your handwritten image data and share it via email. We only collect your handwritten image data of Indus Signs with your data (name, age, and gender). Additionally, we will collect your data where the name consider as a token of user_id, gender (male/female/others), and age (below 20,30,40,50 and so on) via email subject (example: Dave_Jones_Male_34) with image attachments.

How long do we retain your data

We will collect, store, use, and publish that data in research papers and various scientific and other research-related websites for research purposes. We will retain your information for the life that can store online and offline, and that data will be institution property.

What rights do you have over your data

While using our website tool to draw the images, you can only use your handwritten image data for personal use, not commercial or other purposes. After submitting your data to us, you cannot copyright claim on us later. You can request us to delete your data from our database, but you can only do that within 15 days after submitting the data.
