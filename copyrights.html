Privacy Policy and Copyrights Concern

Who we are

Our website address is https://indusdraw.tech. IndusDraw is a project of the Takama Laboratory, Tokyo Metropolitan University.

What personal data do we collect and why we collect it

We are not using cookies embedded with any kind of additional third-party content and do not monitor your interaction to collect your personal information. When you visit the website, you can draw and download your handwritten image data and share it via email. We only collect your handwritten image data of Indus Signs with your personal information (age and gender). Additionally, we will collect your data where the gender considers (male/female/others) and age (below 20,30,40,50 and so on) with email subject (example: Male_34) and image attachments.

How long do we retain your data

The collected data will be used anonymously. The collected data will be provided as an open data after being anonymized.

What rights do you have over your data

While using our website tool to draw the images, you can only use your handwritten image data for personal use, not commercial or other purposes. After submitting your data to us, you cannot copyright claim on us later.

Changes to the Privacy Policy and Copyrights Concern

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when posted on this page.

Security of Your Personal Data

The security of Your Personal Data is important to Us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect Your Personal Data, We cannot guarantee its absolute security.
